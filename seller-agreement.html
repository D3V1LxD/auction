1. Agreement Overview

This Seller Agreement ("Agreement") supplements our Terms of Service and governs your activities as a seller on the AuctionHub platform. By listing items for sale, you agree to comply with all provisions outlined in this Agreement.

Seller Commitment

As a seller on AuctionHub, you commit to providing excellent customer service, accurate item descriptions, and timely fulfillment of all completed sales.

2. Seller Obligations

2.1 General Responsibilities

As a seller, you must:

  • Provide accurate, complete, and honest descriptions of all items
  • Upload clear, high-quality photographs showing the item's actual condition
  • Honor all completed sales and binding commitments
  • Ship items promptly within stated handling times
  • Respond to buyer inquiries within 24 hours
  • Maintain professional communication with buyers
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Keep your account information current and accurate

2.2 Item Ownership and Rights

You warrant that you:

  • Own or have the legal right to sell all listed items
  • Have not encumbered the items with liens or other restrictions
  • Will transfer clear title to buyers upon payment
  • Have not listed stolen, counterfeit, or illegally obtained items

3. Listing Requirements

3.1 Item Descriptions

All item descriptions must include:

  • Accurate title that describes the item
  • Detailed description including dimensions, materials, and condition
  • Clear disclosure of any flaws, damage, or repairs
  • Honest assessment of item condition
  • Relevant technical specifications where applicable
  • Provenance or history when relevant to value

3.2 Photography Requirements

Item photographs must:

  • Show the actual item being sold
  • Be clear, well-lit, and in focus
  • Include multiple angles and detail shots
  • Accurately represent the item's condition and color
  • Highlight any flaws or damage
  • Not use stock photos or images from other sources

3.3 Pricing and Reserve

When setting prices:

  • Starting bids should be reasonable and competitive
  • Reserve prices must be disclosed to potential bidders
  • Buy It Now prices should reflect fair market value
  • All prices must be in the currency specified on our platform

4. Prohibited Items

Strict Prohibition

Listing prohibited items may result in immediate account suspension and legal action. When in doubt, contact our support team before listing.

The following items are strictly prohibited:

  • Illegal Items: Drugs, weapons, stolen goods, counterfeit products
  • Regulated Items: Prescription medications, medical devices, pesticides
  • Dangerous Items: Explosives, hazardous chemicals, radioactive materials
  • Restricted Items: Tobacco, alcohol (in many jurisdictions), fireworks
  • Intellectual Property: Bootleg recordings, unauthorized reproductions
  • Adult Content: Sexually explicit materials, adult services
  • Live Animals: Pets, livestock, endangered species
  • Human Parts: Organs, blood, hair, body parts
  • Services: Labor, consulting, or intangible services

5. Auction Management

5.1 Auction Settings

Sellers are responsible for:

  • Setting appropriate auction duration (1-10 days)
  • Choosing suitable starting bid amounts
  • Determining shipping costs and handling times
  • Specifying accepted payment methods
  • Setting clear return policies

5.2 During the Auction

While your auction is active:

  • Monitor for buyer questions and respond promptly
  • Do not contact bidders to influence their bidding
  • Cannot modify core auction details once bids are placed
  • May add additional information to clarify descriptions
  • Must honor all legitimate bids received

5.3 Auction Cancellation

Auctions may only be cancelled in exceptional circumstances:

  • Item is no longer available due to damage or loss
  • Significant error in listing discovered
  • Legal issues prevent the sale

Frivolous cancellations may result in seller fees and account restrictions.

6. Fees and Payment

6.1 Fee Structure

Fee Type Amount When Charged
Listing Fee Free for most categories When listing is created
Final Value Fee 10% of sale price When item sells
Payment Processing 3% of transaction When payment is processed
Featured Listing $5.00 - $25.00 Optional upgrade fee

6.2 Payment Processing

We process payments as follows:

  • Buyers pay AuctionHub directly for all purchases
  • We hold funds until shipping confirmation or delivery
  • Seller proceeds are released within 2-5 business days
  • Fees are automatically deducted from proceeds
  • Payments are made via your chosen payout method

7. Shipping and Handling

7.1 Shipping Requirements

Sellers must:

  • Ship items within the stated handling time
  • Package items securely to prevent damage
  • Provide tracking information when available
  • Insure high-value items during shipping
  • Upload shipping confirmation to our platform

7.2 Shipping Costs

Shipping charges should:

  • Reflect actual shipping costs
  • Include reasonable packaging expenses
  • Not be used to circumvent final value fees
  • Be clearly stated in the listing

7.3 International Shipping

For international sales, sellers are responsible for:

  • Understanding export/import restrictions
  • Completing customs documentation accurately
  • Declaring proper values for customs
  • Communicating potential customs fees to buyers

8. Returns and Refunds

8.1 Return Policy

Sellers may choose to offer returns with these guidelines:

  • Return policy must be clearly stated in listings
  • Return periods typically range from 7-30 days
  • Items must be returned in original condition
  • Return shipping costs are typically buyer's responsibility
  • Refunds should be processed within 3 business days of receipt

8.2 Mandatory Returns

Returns are required when:

  • Item is significantly not as described
  • Item is damaged during shipping (if insured)
  • Wrong item was sent to buyer
  • Item is discovered to be counterfeit or stolen

9. Seller Protection

9.1 Fraud Protection

We protect sellers from:

  • Fraudulent payment reversals when shipping is confirmed
  • Buyer claims when items are as described
  • Unreasonable return requests outside stated policies
  • Harassment or abusive buyer behavior

9.2 Dispute Resolution

Our dispute resolution process:

  • Encourages direct communication between parties
  • Provides mediation services for unresolved issues
  • Reviews evidence from both parties fairly
  • Makes binding decisions when necessary
  • May provide compensation in cases of platform error

10. Account Management

10.1 Account Standing

We monitor seller performance through:

  • Buyer feedback and ratings
  • Transaction completion rates
  • Response time to buyer inquiries
  • Shipping and handling timeliness
  • Policy compliance history

10.2 Performance Standards

To maintain good standing, sellers should:

  • Maintain 95%+ positive feedback rating
  • Complete 98%+ of all sales
  • Respond to inquiries within 24 hours
  • Ship within stated handling times
  • Resolve disputes professionally

11. Legal Compliance

11.1 Tax Obligations

Sellers are responsible for:

  • Reporting income from sales as required by law
  • Collecting and remitting applicable sales taxes
  • Maintaining records of all transactions
  • Complying with local business licensing requirements

11.2 Regulatory Compliance

Sellers must comply with:

  • Consumer protection laws
  • Truth in advertising regulations
  • Product safety standards
  • Import/export restrictions
  • Intellectual property laws

12. Violations and Enforcement

12.1 Warning System

Policy violations may result in:

  • Email warnings for minor infractions
  • Listing removal for policy violations
  • Temporary selling restrictions
  • Account suspension for serious violations
  • Permanent account termination for severe or repeated violations

12.2 Appeal Process

Sellers may appeal enforcement actions by:

  • Contacting our seller support team
  • Providing documentation supporting their position
  • Requesting review by senior support staff
  • Implementing corrective measures as requested

13. Seller Support

We provide comprehensive support including:

  • 24/7 seller support helpline
  • Online knowledge base and tutorials
  • Best practices guides and selling tips
  • Regular seller newsletters and updates
  • Community forums for seller interaction
  • Account management tools and analytics

14. Agreement Changes

We may modify this Agreement with 30 days' notice to sellers. Significant changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notifications to registered sellers
  • Platform announcements and banners
  • Updates to our seller resource center
  • In-app notifications for active users

15. Contact Information

For seller-specific questions and support:

  • Phone Support: 1-800-SELLERS (24/7)
  • Live Chat: Available in your seller dashboard
  • Mail: AuctionHub Seller Support, 123 Auction Street, Commerce City, ST 12345
