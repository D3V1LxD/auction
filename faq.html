Bidding & Auctions

How do I place a bid on an auction?

To place a bid:

  • Click on the auction item you're interested in
  • Click the "Bid Now" button
  • Enter your maximum bid amount
  • Confirm your bid

Our system will automatically bid for you up to your maximum amount. You'll receive notifications if you're outbid.

What is proxy bidding?

Proxy bidding is our automatic bidding system. When you enter a maximum bid, our system will bid incrementally on your behalf up to that amount, ensuring you always have the lowest winning bid possible.

For example, if the current bid is $50 and you set a maximum of $100, the system might bid $55 for you. If someone else bids $60, the system will automatically bid $65 on your behalf, and so on, until your maximum is reached.

Can I retract a bid once it's placed?

Bids are generally binding and cannot be retracted. However, you may retract a bid in exceptional circumstances:

  • You made a significant error in your bid amount
  • The item description has been significantly changed
  • You cannot reach the seller

To request a bid retraction, contact our support team immediately with the auction ID and reason for retraction.

How do I know if I've won an auction?

You'll be notified immediately when you win an auction through:

  • Email notification
  • On-site notification
  • Your account dashboard

You'll then need to complete payment within 48 hours to secure your purchase.

Selling Items

How do I list an item for auction?

To list an item:

  • Go to our Sell Your Item page
  • Fill out the item details and upload photos
  • Set your starting price and auction duration
  • Review and publish your listing

Our team will review your listing within 24 hours before it goes live.

What fees do I pay as a seller?

Our seller fees are transparent and competitive:

  • Listing fee: FREE for your first 50 listings per month
  • Final value fee: 8% of the final sale price
  • Payment processing: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction
  • Optional features: Featured listings, reserve prices (additional fees apply)

View detailed fee structure in our Seller Agreement.

Can I set a reserve price?

Yes! A reserve price is the minimum amount you're willing to accept for your item. Key points:

  • Reserve prices are hidden from bidders
  • The item won't sell unless the reserve is met
  • Reserve fee: $5 for items under $500, $10 for items over $500
  • Fee is refunded if your item sells

How do I get paid when my item sells?

Payment is processed automatically:

  • Buyer payment is held in escrow until item is delivered
  • Funds are released to you after successful delivery confirmation
  • Payment methods: PayPal, bank transfer, or check
  • Processing time: 1-3 business days after release

Payments & Billing

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various secure payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
  • PayPal
  • Bank transfers (ACH)
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay
  • Cash on pickup (local transactions only)

All payments are processed securely through encrypted connections.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard security measures:

  • SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS compliant payment processing
  • No credit card numbers stored on our servers
  • Two-factor authentication available
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

What if there's a payment dispute?

Our dispute resolution process protects both buyers and sellers:

  • Contact the other party first to resolve the issue
  • If unresolved, open a dispute in your account dashboard
  • Our mediation team will investigate within 48 hours
  • We may hold funds in escrow until resolution
  • Final decisions are based on evidence and platform policies

Shipping & Delivery

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping costs vary by item and location:

  • Costs are calculated by the seller based on item size, weight, and destination
  • Shipping calculator available during checkout
  • Some sellers offer free shipping (included in item price)
  • Local pickup option may be available to avoid shipping costs

How can I track my shipment?

Tracking your order is easy:

  • Login to your account and go to "My Purchases"
  • Click on the order you want to track
  • Tracking number and carrier information will be provided
  • You'll receive email updates on shipping status

What if my item arrives damaged?

If your item arrives damaged:

  • Take photos of the damage and packaging immediately
  • Contact the seller within 48 hours
  • File a dispute if the seller doesn't respond
  • We may require you to get a shipping damage report
  • Refunds or replacements will be handled case-by-case

Account & Profile

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is quick and free:

  • Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
  • Provide your email address and create a password
  • Verify your email address
  • Complete your profile information
  • Add payment methods for bidding/selling

How do I verify my account?

Account verification increases trust and may be required for certain activities:

  • Email verification: Click the link sent to your email
  • Phone verification: Enter the code sent via SMS
  • Identity verification: Upload government ID (for selling)
  • Address verification: Provide proof of address

Verified accounts can participate in higher-value auctions and have better visibility.

Can I change my username?

Username changes are limited to maintain trust and consistency:

  • New accounts: Can change username within first 30 days
  • Established accounts: One username change per year
  • Contact support to request a username change
  • Some restrictions apply (profanity, impersonation, etc.)

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account at any time:

  • Go to Account Settings > Privacy & Security
  • Click "Delete Account"
  • Complete any pending transactions first
  • Confirm deletion via email
  • Account data is permanently removed within 30 days

Note: This action cannot be undone. Consider deactivating instead if you might return.
